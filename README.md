## Using Third-Party Players With PikPak

A question that comes up a lot: can you use VLC, MX Player, or other external players instead of PikPak's built-in player?

**Yes, on Android.** Starting from PikPak Android App version 1.21, there's a "Play with other apps" option in the extended menu for any video file. On iOS, direct handoff to external players is more limited compared to Android, though some WebDAV setups can bridge this.

The **WebDAV route** is the most powerful option for power users. PikPak supports WebDAV access, which means you can point any WebDAV-compatible media player on your network — Infuse, VLC, nPlayer, and many others — directly at your PikPak library. No file-by-file sharing required. Your whole library shows up like a local network drive.
